plocha utvaru ohraniceneho krivkou funkce $\ln x$,
osou $x$ a primkou $x=e$

\section*{reseni}
$\int \ln x ~dx = x(\ln x -1)+C$\\
$\int_1^e \ln x ~dx = e(\ln e -1) - 1(\ln 1 -1)$\\
$\int_1^e \ln x ~dx = e(1 -1) - (0 -1)$\\
$\int_1^e \ln x ~dx = 1$\\
